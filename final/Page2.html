Effects of Global Warming

The fallout of global warming shows up in different forms and becomes increasingly severe: sea levels rise, we have been hit by more extreme weather incidents and the ongoing extinctions of various species. These interfaces bring role changes to ecosystems, human health, and economies everywhere.

"Global warming isn't a prediction. It is happening." - James Hansen

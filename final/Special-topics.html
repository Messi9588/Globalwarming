Special Topics

Web design should not only take proper authorship into account but also makes it a rule to observe the legal usage of resources. These licenses are able to provide creators and authors with the needed range in conditions and permissions.

Accessibility is a basic factor that is made to ensure equal use of the web contents for everyone, including the people with disabilities. For example, complying with rules like WCAG enables websites to be more inclusive for people with disabilities.

Ethical design is the practice wherein a user is in focused be it in the area of privacy, fairness's and accessibility instead. We aim at designing every digital product that takes into account human dignity and works for the better of the world.
